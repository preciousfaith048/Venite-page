Admission Requirements

At Venite, we seek ambitious minds displaying outstanding intellectual potential, extracurricular drive, and leadership qualities. Here is what is expected:

Academic Credentials

High school transcript with credit passes in at least five subjects including English and Mathematics.

Grade Point Benchmark

A minimum GPA equivalent of 2.5 on a 5.0 scale for undergraduate; upper-second for postgraduate.

Personal Statement

A 500-word essay articulating your research aspirations, goals, and desire to join Venite.
